// OBJECTIVE

Accumulate wealth ($USDT) and $WICK tokens by predicting market movements. Survive as long as possible.

// GAMEPLAY LOOP

  • JOIN A BUS: Select a tier based on your risk appetite.
  • BET: Predict if the next candle will close LONG (Green) or SHORT (Red).
  • WIN: Earn $USDT and keep your Integrity.
  • LOSE: Take damage to your Skin Integrity.

// SURVIVAL MECHANICS

  • INTEGRITY: Your life force. If it hits 0, your Skin is BURNED (NFT destroyed).
  • REPAIR: Use earned $WICK tokens to repair your Skin before it's too late.
  • SKINS: Higher level skins have more Integrity but cost more to repair.
